Things to Do

The West Mountain Inn

Join us for a walk throughout the 150 acres of woodland paths on The West Mountain Inn's property, or head over to the barn on site and feed the Alpacas!

Northshire Bookstore

Amazing local bookstore with options for everyone! They also have great local Vermont gifts!

Bonnet & Main Cafe

Located inside Northshire Bookstore and has some of the best coffee around.

The Vermont Flannel Company

Classic stop if you're in Vermont!

Manchester Woodcraft

Amazing hidden gem where you can find loads of handmade wood crafts from cheese boards to handmade Christmas ornaments!

Manchester Designer Outlets;

Some great outlet shops are around Manchester including Le Creuset, J Crew, and Ralph Lauren.

Village Peddler and Chocolatorium

For all those chocolate lovers, this place has it all!

Mystic Restaurant and Bar

Great restaurant with farm fresh comfort food and craft cocktails.

Spa at Equinox

If you're looking to relax, the spa at Equinox Resort has a sauna, pools, and massage packages.

Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

When is the wedding?

The ceremony and reception will be held on May 4th, 2024. We'd love for you to join us for a full weekend of events, including a Welcome Dinner on May 3rd, and a Farewell Brunch on May 5th.

What is the address of the wedding?

All events including the ceremony and reception will be held at The West Mountain Inn, 144 W Mountain Inn Rd, Arlington, VT 05250.

Will transportation be provided?

Yes! We will provide transportation on May 4th from the Hampton Inn in Manchester, VT to The West Mountain Inn and back, for the ceremony and reception. Pick up and drop off times are to be determined.

Will the wedding be outdoors?

The wedding weekend will consist of events both outdoors and indoors. Please keep in mind that it will be early Spring in Vermont. Wet grass and cooler weather is expected.

What is the dress code?

Formal attire. We ask that men wear suits and ties, and women wear a full length dress or similar.
For all other wedding weekend events, we ask that you dress business casual.
